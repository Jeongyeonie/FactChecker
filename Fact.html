Claim: "The earth is flat."

Verdict: False

Claim: "Vaccines cause autism."

Verdict: False

Extensive research has shown no link between vaccines and autism. This claim has been thoroughly discredited by the scientific community.

Claim: "Climate change is a hoax."

Verdict: False

Climate change is a scientifically proven phenomenon supported by a vast body of research. It is not a hoax.
